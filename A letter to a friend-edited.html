03/08/2017


Dear Sam,

I had been meaning to write to you for quite some time. I apologize for the delay. Work has been busy, and I’ve been taking some remedial classes on the side to bring up my marks in time for spring applications.

The weather has been changing for the better, I’ve noticed. These days, the thick snow is just scattered slush, and my winter gear feels too tight and over-warm. You know how much I hate being cold, so it's a welcome change.

Do you remember when we walked the long way home from the theatre so you could see the first snowfall? I was so cold my chest was rattling, but you seemed to enjoy yourself.

I stay off social media these days, and have been trying to do more things with my hands. Writing, reading, even pottery, ha. I didn’t have much time for those things before, so it’s been nice to get back to them.

I’d love to know what you’re up to these days, I can only imagine the fun you’re having.

Do you still save time to go bike riding in the mornings? Has your caffeine addiction gotten worse?

I hope to hear from you soon.

I miss you.


Your friend,

Leah
